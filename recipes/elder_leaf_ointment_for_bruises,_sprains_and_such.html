Elder leaf ointment for bruises, sprains and such

Recipe Page


Go To Home Page

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 handfulls of chopped elderberry leaves
  • 1 oz beeswax
  • Jars with lids
  • Labels
Warm 1 cup extra virgin olive oil in a pan and add a couple of hanfulls of chopped elder leaves.
Simmer gently until the leaves are crip and then strain, returning the oil back into the pan.
Melt one oz. beeswax in the oil and pour into ointment hjars.
Cool, label, and put on lids.
