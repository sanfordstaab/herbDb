Elderberry glycerite for sore throats and coughs

Recipe Page


Go To Home Page


Ingredients:
  • Elder flowers
  • vegetable glycerine
  • bottles
  • labels
Pack the heads of elder flowers into a large jar and fill with vegetabele glycerine.
Stir to release any air bubbles.
Put the lid on and place the jar in a warm place for two weeks.
Strain.
Bottle the liquid, label and store in a cool dark place.
Use one tsp. as needed for sore throats.
