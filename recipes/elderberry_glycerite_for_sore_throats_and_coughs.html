Elderberry glycerite for sore throats and coughs

Recipe Page


Go To Home Page

Ingredients:
  • vegetable glycerine
  • Jars with lids
  • labels
Fill a large jar with elderberries and pour vegetable glycerin over them until the jar is full.
Place in a warm spot for two weeks.
Strain and squeeze the juice from the berries.
Bottle the liquid, label and store in a cool, dark place.
Use one tsp. as needed for sore throats.
