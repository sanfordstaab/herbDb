Herb Recipes:

For colds and flus: place two tsp. dried elder flowers in one cup of hot water three times daily.

For hay fever and sinusitis: use one tsp. each of dried leaves and flowers per one cup of hto water three times daily. If using a tincture, use two tsp. two times daily throughout the winter months.
